tamil recepie
Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic, spices, salt for 1 hour.
​
Sauté onions, add tomatoes, marinated chicken; cook until tender, add mint/coriander.
​
Parboil soaked rice with whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom); layer over gravy, add ghee/water, dum cook 10-15 mins on low flame.

Serve hot with raita.
